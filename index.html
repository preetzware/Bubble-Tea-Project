Bubble Tea: A brief overview

Bubble tea, commonly known as boba tea, is a Taiwanese drink that has taken the world by storm. It originated in the 1980s and has since become a global phenomenon, with stores opening in every major city worldwide. The drink mainly combines tea, milk, and tapioca pearls, creating a unique and flavorful experience. In recent years, bubble tea culture has continued to evolve, with new trends and flavours emerging. One of the most notable trends is using natural ingredients in bubble tea, such as fresh fruits and herbs. This has led to a new focus on health and wellness in the bubble tea industry, with many shops, including Bobo Tea offering healthier options free from artificial flavours and preservatives.
